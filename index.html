About DevDay

IOSD's DevDay will cover multiple aspects of Open Source Development such as Android, Firebase, Google Cloud Platform, Microsoft Azure, Google Assistant, Flutter, Google Summer of Code and much more. We would be discussing the progress of the organisation as a whole and the happenings in each individual student chapter, with major focus on our new open-source projects and drive to get every members and student in the community on a project or internship. Events will include Speaker Sessions, Workshops, Community Outreach and trainings with mentors from Google Developers Experts, Women Techmaker Ambassadors, Github Campus Experts and Advisers. Featuring the Top Prodigies from India's Leading Software Development based Organisation, the meetup will be an invite exclusive event.
